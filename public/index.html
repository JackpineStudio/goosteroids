The "grey goo" of the internet has come alive in this remake of the classic Asteroids game!
Globs of goo are attracted to one another and clump together to form blobs.
You must destroy these globs and blobs, before they take over the universe, by shooting them with your spaceship's laser!
Blobs fly apart when shot, and the force of attraction between the globs results in fast, crazy, and unpredictable motion!
